STEP 2

Connect Wallet

2

Once you’ve connected your wallet, you’ll be presented with 3 options with which you can purchase DOGO token.

Read below for more information on these three options, and select the option which is right for you.

Buy with ETH

If you have enough ETH in your wallet, you can use the purchase DOGO with ETH option in order to swap the ETH in your wallet for DOGO.

Type in the amount of DOGO you wish to purchase and then click “Convert ETH”. Your wallet provider will ask you to confirm the transaction and will also show you the cost of gas.

Buy with USDT

Before you start trading, make sure you have at least $30 USDT in your wallet. Enter the amount of DOGO you wish to purchase (minimum 20,000). Click "Convert USDT".

You will then be asked to approve the purchase twice. The first approval is for the USDT contract and the second approval is for the transaction amount. Please make sure you go through two approval steps to complete the transaction.

WHAT IS $DOGO TOKEN?

Multi-Utility Meme Coin

The native token of the Dogodoge ecosystem, $DOGO is an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum network and utility token of the Dogoverse, it can be used for both utility of purchase NFTs, participate in game modes, P2E and governance.   

DOGO will have a total supply of 10 billion DOGO tokens – similar to other altcoins which also has a 10 billion max supply.

Players will be able to convert their DOGO tokens earned in-game into any other currency using the Dogo Swap DEX, a decentralized exchange integrated into all Dogo game arenas, or choose to hold and stake DOGO – stakers are also eligible for extra benefits within the game.
